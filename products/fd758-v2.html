Key Features

Engineered
to endure

01
High Driving Power

Aggressive lug pattern with deep 21 mm tread depth delivers superior traction on loose, unpaved surfaces — moving heavier loads with less wheel slip. The wide shoulder lugs distribute torque evenly under full load cycling.

02
Puncture Resistance

Reinforced 4-belt all-steel construction and stone-ejector grooves shield the casing from sharp debris common in mining and quarry environments. Tested to exceed standard penetration resistance thresholds by 30%.

03
Anti Block-Drop

Interlocking shoulder block design and high-tear compound prevent chunking and tread loss when operating across sharp rock and broken pavement. Compound formulation tuned for cut-and-chip resistance in extreme temperature ranges.

04
Wide Lateral Grooves

Deep cross-channels evacuate mud and water at speed, sustaining grip on slick mining haul roads and waterlogged construction sites where hydroplaning risk is high.

Common Questions

Before you order

FD758 is built for medium to heavy-duty trucks in off-road and mixed conditions — typically 6×4 / 8×4 dump trucks, mixers, and mining haul trucks. It is engineered for the drive axle position where torque transfer and traction are critical.
315/80R22.5 (PR20) carries up to 4000 kg single / 3650 kg dual at 900 kPa, rated for 120 km/h (Speed L). 12R22.5 (PR18) carries 3550 kg single / 3250 kg dual at 830 kPa, rated for 110 km/h (Speed K). Always observe local axle-load regulations.
Field data indicates 60,000–80,000 km on typical mining and construction routes. Mileage varies significantly with road severity, axle alignment, and inflation discipline. Highway-heavy routes will see the upper end; pure mining will sit lower.
FD758 is optimized for unpaved and mixed routes. For long-haul highway service, we recommend our FD160 (drive) or FA999 (all-position) patterns, which deliver lower rolling resistance and longer mileage on smooth pavement.
