FRIDERIC all-steel radial tyres are engineered for heavy-duty commercial vehicles — long-haul semi-trucks, regional distribution trucks, dump trucks, concrete mixers, and mining haul trucks. Pattern selection (steer, drive, trailer, all-position) determines the right fitment for each axle.
Long Haul patterns (FA999, RS109, FA808, FA818, FA131) prioritise low rolling resistance and high mileage on smooth motorways. Regional patterns (FD758, RD518, FD160, FD163) balance highway and mixed-surface performance with stronger sidewalls and deeper tread. Mining & Off-Road patterns (FD165) use heavy-ply construction and aggressive tread blocks for unpaved haul roads and construction sites.
Our products carry CCC (China), DOT (USA), ECE R117 (EU), INMETRO (Brazil), GSO/GCC (Gulf States), SNI (Indonesia), BIS (India), SASO (Saudi Arabia), and TISI (Thailand). Certification coverage varies by pattern and size — contact our team for the exact list for your destination market.
Standard MOQ is 1 × 40HQ container — approximately 200–240 pieces depending on size. For trial orders or mixed-size containers, our team can discuss flexible arrangements. OEM / private-label orders typically require a minimum of 1,000 pieces per pattern.
