18-station automatic batching system ensures precise compound formulation for consistent tire quality.
Advanced calendering lines apply rubber compounds onto steel cord fabric with precision thickness control.
Skilled operators assemble tire components on building machines, forming the green tire with exact specifications.
High-pressure vulcanization at controlled temperatures cures the rubber, giving tires their final strength and tread pattern.
X-ray inspection detects internal structural defects invisible to the naked eye, ensuring every tire meets safety standards.
Tensile strength and endurance testing machines verify load capacity, ensuring tires withstand extreme real-world conditions.
Precision steel cord calendering produces reinforcement layers that give our tires superior strength and puncture resistance.

Our Story
Founded in Guangrao, Shandong — the "Tire Capital of China" — HUGER has grown from a local rubber factory into a global tire manufacturer. With 1.8 billion yuan in total assets, three flagship brands, and products certified across 10+ international standards, we are committed to building safer, greener roads worldwide.
